Virtual Krewe of Vaporwave Bylaws

Article I: Introduction

Section 1.1: Preamble

Greetings, Virtual Krewe of Vaporwave members! In these concise bylaws, we outline the rules and principles guiding our creative chaos.

Article II: Governance Tokens and Voting

Section 2.1: Acquisition of Governance Tokens

Krewe members obtain governance tokens through dues payments or using Wavebucks, promoting equity and honoring their creative contributions.

Section 2.2: Voting and Decision-Making

Our decision-making blends creative anarchy with structure, preferring consensus but employing the Ask the Oracle method when consensus wanes. The AI secretary synthesizes member inputs into binding decisions, promoting transparency and informed choices.

Article III: Treasurer and Wavebucks

Section 3.1: Treasurer's Responsibilities

The treasurer oversees Wavebucks issuance for Krewe project labor and sets the labor-to-Wavebucks exchange rate, motivating participation and acknowledging creative contributions.

Section 3.2: Wavebucks

Wavebucks, our digital currency, represent the value of labor within the Krewe. They can be earned through contributions to projects, facilitating the exchange of creativity within our community. Wavebucks can be converted into other Krewe assets, like governance tokens or Mardi Gras tickets, at exchange rates determined collectively.

Article IV: Exchange of Wavebucks and Krewe Assets

Section 4.1: Exchange Rate of Wavebucks

Equitable exchange rates between Wavebucks and Krewe assets are defined through member proposals and votes, nurturing an ecosystem where our digital currency possesses genuine value. Transparency and equity underpin this process.

Article V: Manager of the AI Secretary

Section 5.1: Election and Responsibilities

Elections determine the AI secretary manager, tasked with inputting data and recording AI outputs. The Ask the Oracle method aids in decision-making, upholding transparent and collaborative governance.

Article VI: The Ask the Oracle Method

Section 6.1: The Ask the Oracle Method

In times when consensus eludes us, we turn to the Ask the Oracle method, inspired by the ancient Greek tradition of seeking wisdom. Members submit their preferences to our AI secretary, which anonymously synthesizes these inputs into a binding decision. This approach harnesses collective wisdom while maintaining individual privacy.

Article VII: Miscellaneous Provisions

Section 7.1: Interpretation

We offer guidelines for interpreting the bylaws and resolving disputes and ensure remaining parts stay enforceable.

Section 7.2: Severability

If one part is unenforceable, the rest still apply.

Section 7.3: Effective Date

The bylaws take effect as indicated.

Article VIII: Acknowledgment and Ratification

Section 8.1: Acknowledgment and Ratification

Krewe members acknowledge and ratify these bylaws.
